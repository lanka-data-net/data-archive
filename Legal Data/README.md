LankaData hosts a structured digital repository of Sri Lankan legal datasets, including Acts of Parliament and Ordinances from independence to date, consolidated statutes, the Constitution, and key legislative texts, together with extensive case law such as Sri Lanka Law Reports, New Law Reports, Supreme Court and Court of Appeal judgments, historical decisions, and legal digests. The platform also includes legal publications, case summaries, and international treaties and conventions relevant to Sri Lanka, all organized for searchable and reliable digital access.
